Rakhi for Soldiers 2024
19 Aug 2024

The Rakhi for Soldiers program is a heartfelt initiative aimed at recognizing and honoring the sacrifices of our brave soldiers. Through this program, we aim to strengthen the bond between civilians and the armed forces by celebrating the festival of Raksha Bandhan with those who protect our nation.
This acceptance from the Hon'ble Governor is a significant endorsement of our efforts and will undoubtedly inspire many to join us in this endeavor. We look forward to his participation and the positive impact it will have on the morale of our soldiers and the community at large.
